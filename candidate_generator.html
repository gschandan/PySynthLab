Recursively expand an expression using the weighted grammar.

This method generates all possible expansions of a given expression according to the grammar rules, up to a maximum depth. It also calculates and propagates weights for each expansion.

Parameters:

  • expr (Union[str, tuple]) – The expression to expand.

List[Tuple[z3.ExprRef, int]]
